Scam Email Classifier

This web app will tell you if the text input that you give it is a specific type of email scam colloquially known as the Nigerian Prince Scam.
Scams of this type typically involve someone from a foreign country reaching out to their target with a "business proposal" that involves them asking if they can deposit a large sum of money into their target's bank account. This is a scam to get the person's bank account information.
